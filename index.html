Welcome to my website.

I am a Research Fellow in the Cyber Security Centre (CSC), De Montfort University, Leicester.

I am interested in addressing issues, such as usability and privacy, in the interaction between us and our data. My PhD focused on applying machine-learning techniques to recommending location-privacy preferences. My current research is about understanding the strength of privacy metrics in different scenarios.

If you want to know more about what I do, please check my papers!

Before joining the CSC to work with Dr Isabel Wagner , I did my PhD in the School of Computer Science at the University of St Andrews, under the supervision of Dr Tristan Henderson and Dr Juan Ye.
