Welcome to my website.

I am a Research Associate in the Systems and Algorithms Laboratory (SysAL), Dyson School of Design and Engineering at Imperial College London.

I am interested in addressing issues between people and their data. For example, my PhD focused on using recommender systems to help people manage the access to their location data.

If you want to know more about what I do, please check my papers!

I did my PhD in the School of Computer Science at the University of St Andrews, under the supervision of Dr Tristan Henderson and Dr Juan Ye. Before joining the SysAL to work with Dr Hamed Haddadi , I worked at the University of Southampton and De Montfort University, Leicester, respectively
