Welcome to my website.

I am a Research Fellow in the Web and Internet Science (WAIS) Research Group, School of Electronics and Computer Science (ECS) at the University of Southampton.

I am interested in addressing issues between people and their data. For example, my PhD focused on using recommender systems to help people manage the access to their location data. Currently I am interested in designing novel tools to visualise and tell stories about data.

If you want to know more about what I do, please check my papers!

I did my PhD in the School of Computer Science at the University of St Andrews, under the supervision of Dr Tristan Henderson and Dr Juan Ye. Before joining the WAIS to work with Prof Elena Simperl , I worked as a Research Fellow in the Cyber Security Centre (CSC) at the De Montfort University, Leicester.
